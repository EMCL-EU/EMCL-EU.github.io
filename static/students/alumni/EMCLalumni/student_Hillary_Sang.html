Current work:
I teach Linguistics. My teaching subjects include: The Structure of Language, Suprasegmental Phonetics and Phonology and Introduction to Semantics. I am currently developing a curriculum for the introduction of Clinical Linguistic studies at the department through a collaboration with Linköping University. My research interests explore cross-linguistic aphasia, phonology of African languages with the aim of writing about some of the features of African languages that have not been studied or explored extensively.
