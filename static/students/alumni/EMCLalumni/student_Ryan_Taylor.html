Current work:
I am interested in speech and language in use, in context, from speech control up to the physical and cultural context of language use. Most recently, my interests have turned toward the effect of technology on communication breakdown and strategies to overcome this breakdown, the influence of paralinguistic communication signals like prosody and facial expressions on speech production and interpretation, in addition to the development and refinement of research methodology.
