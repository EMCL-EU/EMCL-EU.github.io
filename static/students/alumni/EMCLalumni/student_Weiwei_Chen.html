Current work:
Right now I am engaged in teaching young children with autism through various training programs. These few months most of my work is one-to-one interaction with them, using DTT, PECS, Melieu Teaching and whatever other means of instruction I consider suitable and supportive for each individual, in the hope that they can develop social and motor skills which are displayed by their peers.
