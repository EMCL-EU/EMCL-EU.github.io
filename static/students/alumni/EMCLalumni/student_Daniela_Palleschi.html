Current work:
I use various measures (self-paced reading times, eye-tracking, EEG) to investigate how context modulates processing of verb tense in otherwise grammatical sentences. I'm specifically interested in the English Present Perfect, but am also part of a project that investigates how real-world knowledge is integrated during language processing in German.
