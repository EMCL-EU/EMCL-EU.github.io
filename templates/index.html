The European Master's in Clinical Linguistics is an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree programme already running for over 20 years. Its present edition, EMCL+, was offered by the universities of Groningen, Potsdam (until intake 2018) and Eastern Finland.

It will soon (intake 2021) be superseded by the EMCL++, organised by

  • University of Groningen
  • University of Eastern Finland
  • Ghent University

On this website, you can find more information about the programme, the admission procedure, the associated partners and the modules and courses.

If you want to get in touch with us, please have a look at our contact information.
