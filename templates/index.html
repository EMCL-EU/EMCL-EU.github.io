Welcome to EMCL

The European Master's in Clinical Linguistics is an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree programme already running for over 20 years. The newest edition, EMCL++, is offered by the universities of Groningen, Ghent and Eastern Finland.

Information on applications for EMCL++ and scholarships available can be found here.

EMCL++ offers a 24-month 120 ECTS full-time interdisciplinary and transnational university programme at the Master's level providing integrated training in experimental linguistics, neurolinguistics, psycholinguistics and clinical linguistics. The aim of EMCL++ is to train highly qualified advanced students. Not only are students excellently prepared for research work and PhD programmes in the above fields, they will also be widely employable in the R&D and clinical sector, thanks to the inclusion of representatives from these sectors as associated partners in the programme.
