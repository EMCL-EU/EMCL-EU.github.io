I work under the supervision of Dr. Karsten Steinhauer using behavioural methods and ERPs. My PhD project is related to language-specific and domain-general neurocognitive mechanisms underlying grammar learning in children with Specific Language Impairment. At the same time, I continue working on a project investigating the similarities between language and music.
