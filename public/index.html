The European Master's in Clinical Linguistics is an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree programme already running for over 20 years. Its present edition, EMCL+, was offered by the universities of Groningen, Potsdam (until intake 2018) and Eastern Finland.

It will soon be superseded by the EMCL++, organised by

  • University of Groningen
  • University of Eastern Finland
  • Ghent University
